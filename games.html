Interactive Games

Three games that showcase logic, security awareness, and puzzle‑style thinking — a Wordle-style word game, a phishing spotter, and a cipher‑breaking lab.

Word Quest

Guess the 5-letter tech word in 6 tries. Green = right letter, right spot. Yellow = letter in word, wrong spot. Gray = not in word.

Phishing Shield

Read each email or URL and decide whether you would trust it. Great for practicing real‑world phishing detection.

Cipher Lab

Break simple substitution ciphers, Caesar shifts, and leetspeak to reveal hidden messages — like a miniature capture‑the‑flag lab.
